Questions

+ Is there a refund policy?
7-day 7-day free trial. If dictate.app doesn't fit your workflow, full refund—no questions asked.
+ Where does my data go?
Just Groq. Your audio goes to Groq's Whisper API — the same infrastructure that powers major AI products. Groq does not store your audio or train models on it. Nothing reaches HMW servers. Ever.
+ Does it require internet?
Yes — dictate-app.pages.dev uses Groq's Whisper API for transcription, which requires internet. The upside: Groq is ~300× faster than running Whisper locally on CPU. ~200ms per recording instead of 1-2 seconds. Your audio goes to Groq only — they don't store it or train on it.
+ How do I get started?
Download the app, press the hotkey, speak. Done. No setup, no account, no credit card. Works in any text field on your computer.
+ Can I set my own hotkey?
Yes — any plan, any user. Go to Settings and set any key combination you want. Ctrl+Space is the default, but make it yours.
+ What languages are supported?
70+ languages. Auto-detect or manually pin a language in settings. English, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Arabic, and many more.
+ Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel with one click. No locks, no tricks. Your 7-day trial requires no credit card — you choose at the end.
+ What are the system requirements?
Windows 10+ or macOS 11+. 500MB disk space. Microphone required. 4GB RAM minimum.
