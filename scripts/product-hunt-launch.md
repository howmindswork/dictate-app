## 6. Hunter Strategy

**Recommendation: Self-hunt.**

Here's why: Top hunters add upvotes from their followers, but their followers are often PH regulars who upvote everything and don't convert. Your target is Windows users who dictate — not PH powerusers. A warm hunter matters less for a niche utility app than for a consumer social app.

If you want a hunter anyway, contact these 5 people 2–3 weeks before May 12:

1. **Chris Messina** (@chrismessina) — invented the hashtag, prolific PH hunter, loves dev tools
2. **Kevin William David** (@kwdinc) — hunts productivity and SaaS tools regularly
3. **Bram Kanstein** (@bramk) — Product Hunt OG, hunts no-code and productivity
4. **Ryan Hoover** (@rrhoover) — PH founder, rarely hunts but huge reach if he picks you up
5. **Lenny Rachitsky** (@lennysan) — product/PM focused, hunts tools his audience uses

**How to reach them:** Twitter/X DM only. Short message: "Launching a Windows dictation app on May 12. 7-day free trial, offline Whisper, push-to-talk. Would love a hunter with credibility in dev tools. Happy to give you early access." Attach a GIF of the app in action.

## 7. Upvote Momentum Plan — First 2 Hours Are Critical

PH's algorithm rewards velocity. You need 30+ upvotes in the first 2 hours to enter "trending" territory.

**Who to notify the moment you launch:**

| Channel | Who | Message |
|---------|-----|---------|
| Personal text/iMessage | 10 close friends/family | "Hey — I launched my app on Product Hunt, would mean a lot if you upvoted: [link]" |
| Discord | Indie Hackers Discord, Maker's Kitchen, WIP.co | Drop link, share the story |
| Twitter DMs | Anyone who engaged with dictate.app tweets | Direct ask |
| Email | Your trial/subscriber list | "We're live today — here's the link" |
| Slack | See section 9 | Share in #share-your-work or #show-and-tell channels |

**Golden rule:** Never ask for upvotes in bulk public posts. Always personal, always genuine. PH removes suspicious upvote patterns. People on PH who have never logged in cannot upvote — make sure your friends have PH accounts before launch day.
