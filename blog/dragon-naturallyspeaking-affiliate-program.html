The Bad News: Dragon's Program Is Gone

Dragon's consumer affiliate program ran through Commission Junction (now CJ Affiliate). At its peak, it paid 8% commission on a one-time purchase. That sounds okay until you look at the numbers.

Dragon Home cost around $200. An 8% commission earned you $16 per sale. Dragon Professional was $500, so $40 per sale. Those are one-time payments. If the same customer renewed, bought an upgrade, or purchased another Dragon product - you got nothing.

Then Nuance killed the consumer line. Microsoft acquired Nuance in 2022. Dragon consumer products were quietly discontinued. The affiliate listing on CJ stayed up but stopped converting. New affiliates cannot get approved. Existing affiliates have nothing left to promote.

The Nuance affiliate program has the same problem. Nuance as a standalone brand is gone. Everything folded into Microsoft enterprise offerings. There is no affiliate program for Dragon Medical or Dragon Professional that a typical content creator can access.

If you are still seeing Dragon listed on affiliate networks, those listings are stale. The products are not available for consumer purchase in most markets. The program is not active.
