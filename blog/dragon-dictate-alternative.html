Dragon Requires Voice Training. dictate.app Does Not.

One of the traditional Dragon requirements was an upfront voice profile training session - 20 to 30 minutes of reading passages so the system could calibrate to your voice. It improved accuracy but added friction.

dictate.app requires zero training. Open it. Start dictating. Groq Whisper adapts to your voice in real time.

Dragon's Legacy Bloat

Dragon has accumulated decades of features. Command macros, dictation profiles, PowerMic support, Dragon Anywhere integration. If you need all of that, Dragon is your tool.
