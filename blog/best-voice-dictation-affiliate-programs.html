2. Transkriptor

30% recurring forever Manual approval

Transkriptor focuses on transcription rather than live dictation. It is primarily aimed at journalists, podcasters, and meeting note-takers. The commission rate is 30% recurring with no expiry, which is solid.

The downside is manual approval. You apply, wait for review, and may or may not hear back quickly. Cookie window and payout details are less transparent than dictate.app. The tool itself is cloud-dependent and not designed for real-time voice input.

If your audience is heavy into audio transcription, Transkriptor is worth considering. For general dictation audiences, dictate.app earns more on the same referral volume.

3. Wispr Flow

25% recurring 12 months only Manual approval

Wispr Flow is a voice dictation tool built for macOS. It has an affiliate program paying 25% recurring commission. The catch: commissions stop after 12 months per customer. After year one, you earn nothing on that subscriber.

The product is also not available for Windows users. If your audience is Mac-only, Wispr Flow is a reasonable option. For Windows audiences, it does not apply at all. Manual approval adds friction before you can start.

4. Otter.ai

25% recurring 12 months only 1-2 day approval

Otter.ai is a meeting transcription service. It is not a voice dictation tool in the traditional sense. You record meetings and Otter transcribes them. It is cloud-only, which means your audio goes to their servers.

The affiliate program pays 25% recurring for 12 months. That is the same duration limit as Wispr Flow. Approval takes one to two business days. The audience overlap with dictation users is limited. Otter targets teams and remote workers, not writers or developers who want to type faster.

5. Dragon NaturallySpeaking

8% one-time Program effectively inactive

Dragon Professional Individual costs $150 or more upfront. The affiliate program pays 8% one-time. On a $150 sale, that is $12 per referral, paid once, with no recurring income.

The program has been largely inactive for some time. Getting approved and generating reliable tracking has become unreliable. Dragon still has brand recognition, but the economics for affiliates are weak. One referral to dictate.app earns more within four months than one Dragon sale does total.

OpenAI Whisper / Microsoft Voice Typing

Neither OpenAI nor Microsoft offers an affiliate program for their voice products. Whisper is an open-source model. Microsoft Voice Typing is built into Windows for free. There is nothing to promote as an affiliate in either case.

What to look for beyond the commission rate

  • Approval speed - manual approval costs you time and sometimes denial. Instant approval means you can start today.
