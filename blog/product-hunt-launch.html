Why We Built This

Windows users have been stuck. Wispr Flow is Mac only. Dragon costs $300 and the consumer version is discontinued. Windows built-in dictation drops words and needs an internet connection.

We wanted something simple. Hold a key. Speak. Release. Done.

So we built dictate.app. Push-to-talk hotkey. Groq Whisper under the hood. Transcription in under a second. Works in every app. No internet required.
